# Power-Ups Reference

These systems were removed from the initial MVP to keep the first prototype focused on the core Chopsticks rules. Keep this reference for a later version.

## Game Mode

- Mode label: Powerups
- This mode keeps the stricter split rule where splits cannot revive a hand with 0 chopsticks.
- Standard Game Mode does allow splitting into a 0-chopstick hand.

## Card And Energy Rules

- Each turn allows one normal action and one power-up.
- Max energy is 7.
- Players gain 1 energy automatically at the start of their turn.
- Unused energy carries over.
- Each player has a 20-card deck.
- Starting hand size is 3.
- Max card hand size is 7.
- Used power-ups go to the player's graveyard.
- When the deck is empty, shuffle the graveyard into the deck.
- Only the current player's cards should be visible.
- Instant power-ups can be played during the opponent's turn.

## MVP Deck Composition

- Energy Up x3
- Energy Down x2
- Shield x3
- Precision Strike x3
- Time Freeze x2
- Rebalance x2
- Redirect x2
- Double Tap x1
- Overload x1
- Revive x1

## MVP Power-Ups

### Energy Up

- Cost: 0 energy
- Effect: Gain 1 additional energy at the start of your next turn.

### Energy Down

- Cost: 0 energy
- Effect: Opponent loses 1 energy gain at the start of their next turn.

### Shield

- Cost: 2 energy
- Effect: Choose one of your hands. It blocks the next attack for 1 turn.

### Precision Strike

- Cost: 2 energy
- Effect: Your next attack this turn may count as +1 or -1.

### Time Freeze

- Cost: 3 energy
- Effect: Opponent cannot split or rebalance on their next turn.

### Rebalance

- Cost: 3 energy
- Effect: Redistribute your live chopsticks freely. Cannot revive dead hands.

### Redirect

- Cost: 4 energy
- Type: Instant
- Effect: During the opponent's turn, redirect one attack to your other live hand.

### Double Tap

- Cost: 6 energy
- Effect: Your next attack this turn hits the same target twice.

### Overload

- Cost: 6 energy
- Effect: For this turn, attacks wrap once instead of killing at 5.

### Revive

- Cost: 7 energy
- Effect: Bring one dead hand back with 1 chopstick.
- Limit: Once per game.
