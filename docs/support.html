Support

Need help with Chopsticks & Chai? Use this page for account, gameplay, friend, lobby, chat, or deletion support.

Contact

Email support: ericytchen@gmail.com

When you contact support, include the email address on your account, your display name if you have one, the device you are using, and a short description of the issue.

Account Help

If you cannot sign in, confirm you are using the same email/password account used to create your profile. Password reset support will be added as the app moves toward TestFlight and App Store readiness.

Friend Requests, Lobbies, and Chat

If friend requests, invites, lobbies, or lobby chat do not appear correctly, close and reopen the app first. If the issue continues, contact support with the other player display name, approximate time, and what you expected to happen.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account from inside the app. If deletion fails or you need help, contact support from the email address associated with your account and request account deletion.
