Privacy Policy

Draft for review. Last updated: July 1, 2026.

Chopsticks & Chai is a mobile game that lets players create an account, play matches, invite friends, use lobbies, and send lobby chat messages. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and the choices available to players.

Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect the email address and password credentials needed to create and sign in to your account through Firebase Authentication. We also store gameplay-related account information such as your display name, avatar selection, friend connections, lobby participation, game invites, match/game state, saved progress, and lobby chat messages.

We may also receive technical information automatically from Firebase and the app platform, such as device and app diagnostics, authentication events, security logs, and basic usage information needed to operate, debug, protect, and improve the service.

How We Use Information

We use information to create and secure accounts, keep players signed in, save game progress, support friend requests, create and manage lobbies, deliver game invites, display lobby chat, troubleshoot issues, prevent abuse, and maintain the app.

Firebase and Service Providers

The app uses Google Firebase services, including Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore. These services process information on our behalf to provide account, database, security, and app operation features. Firebase may process technical and diagnostic data according to Google Firebase terms and privacy documentation.

Chat and User Content

Lobby chat messages and other user-generated content may be visible to other players in the same lobby or game context. Do not share sensitive personal information in chat or usernames.

Data Retention and Deletion

We keep account and gameplay data for as long as needed to provide the app, maintain security, resolve issues, and comply with legal obligations. You may delete your account from within the app. Account deletion is intended to remove your Firebase Authentication account and associated app profile data. Some information may remain for a limited time in backups, logs, or records where required for security, troubleshooting, or legal reasons.

Your Choices

You can choose not to create an account, but account features such as online saves, friends, lobbies, and chat may not be available. You can request support or account deletion help using the support page.

Security

We use Firebase security rules and platform security tools to help protect account and gameplay data. No system is perfectly secure, and players should use a unique password and keep their account credentials private.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app changes. When we do, we will update the date above and publish the revised policy.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, contact us through the Chopsticks & Chai Support page.
